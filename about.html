About Us

We are part of El Santo Cantina will offer unique drinks and Mexican style seafood plates with the best atmosphere. After hours, space transforms into a nightclub featuring Latino music. We are sharing business space with Brazilian Steakhouse, which we encourage you to try!

Dress code policy:
  • No sports, recreation or athletic attire
  • No hats, baseball caps or beanies
  • Men are not allowed to wear shorts or tank tops
  • No flannels or flip flops
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to any patron that lacks adequate hygiene (extreme body odor, etc) and or in dress code
